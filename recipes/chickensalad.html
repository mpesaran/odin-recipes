Chicken Salad

Description

This feta chicken salad is a basic chicken salad recipe with red bell peppers and feta cheese. Try using tomato-basil feta for added flavor!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Celery
  • Sour Cream
  • Feta Cheese
  • Capsicum

Steps

  1. Mix chicken, celery, and red onion together in a serving bowl.
  2. Stir mayonnaise, sour cream, feta cheese, and dill together in a separate bowl. Pour over chicken mixture, and stir to blend. Taste, and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  3. Serve immediately, or refrigerate until serving.
